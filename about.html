About Us

LimaSmart was started in the year 2021 as a project for the Microsoft ADC Mentorship Program. For us to work we require that a a soil sample is give then we provide the farmer with the crop that would do well on their soil. Our mission is ensuring that farmers get quality tields and in large quantities. We send newsletters to our members on the tools to use and the current price of the recommended crop in the market.
